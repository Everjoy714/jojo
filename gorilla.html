GORILLA

Gorillas are primarily herbivorous, terrestrial great apes that inhabit the tropical forests of equatorial Africa. The genus Gorilla is divided into two species: the eastern gorilla and the western gorilla,either four or five subspecies.

The largest of the great apes,they are stocky animals with broad chests and shoulders, large, human-like hands, and small eyes set into hairless faces
