ANIMALS MAP PLACES

LION

The lion is a large cat of the genus Panthera, native to Sub-Saharan Africa,India. It has a muscular, broad-chested body; a short, rounded head; round ears; and a dark, hairy tuft at the tip of its tail.

Lions live in a variety of habitats but prefer grassland, savanna, dense scrub, and open woodland

WHERE ARE THEY FOUND?

Lions are primarily found in sub-Saharan Africa, with a small population also residing in India. African lions are native to Africa, south of the Sahara Desert, while Asiatic lions are found in the Gir Forest of western India.
