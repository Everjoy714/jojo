MONKEY TRAIL

The Monkey Trail is a popular hiking path in Ao Nang, Krabi, Thailand, leading to Pai Plong Beach. It's known for being steep, with many steps,offers the chance to see monkeys along the way.

The walk is typically 10-15 minutes each way.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

Most monkeys live in the tropical rainforests of Asia, Africa, and Central and South America, or the savannas of Africa.
