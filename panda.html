ANIMALS MAP PLACES

PANDA

Pandas are black and white bears, native to China, known for their distinctive appearance and their diet of mostly bamboo. They are also excellent tree climbers and good swimmers, and have enlarged wrist bones that act like thumbs to help grasp food.

They are rotund and weigh 100-115 kg (200-300 pounds) as adults.

They have vertical slits for pupils, like domestic cats, according to WWF-UK.

WHERE ARE THEY FOUND?

Giant pandas are primarily found in the temperate forests of mountains in southwestern China, specifically within the Sichuan, Shaanxi, and Gansu provinces
